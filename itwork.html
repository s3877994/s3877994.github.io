IT Work

What kind of work is done by the IT professional?

The person that I interviewed is an IT professor at FPT University. His main job is to teach students about IT knowledge especially about programming language.

What kinds of people does the IT professional interact with? Are they other IT professionals? Clients? Investors? The general public?

His job requires him to interact with other IT professor, his college every day. He also interacts with his students at the University.

Where do the IT professionals spend most of their time?

He spent most of his time in the University to teach his students. At home, he spent most of his time preparing for the lecture and exercise for his students. He also spends a lot of time reading books and news about technology.

What aspect of their position is most challenging?

The biggest challenge of his job is need to improve his knowledge every day by reading books and news about new invention in IT technology because the IT world is developing every day.
